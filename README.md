# Credit_Card_Financial_Dashboard
# Credit Card Weekly Dashboard | Power BI Analytics Project

## Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze credit card transaction data, customer behavior, revenue performance, and weekly business trends.  

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through KPI tracking, customer segmentation, trend analysis, and forecasting techniques. It enables data-driven decision-making by identifying high-performing customer groups, spending patterns, and future revenue opportunities.

# Project Objectives
- Analyze overall revenue and transaction performance
- Monitor customer spending behavior and card usage
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Track activation and delinquency metrics
- Perform weekly and quarterly revenue trend analysis
- Apply forecasting techniques for future revenue prediction
- Generate business insights and strategic recommendations

---

# Key Business Insights

- Blue Card customers contribute nearly **83% of total revenue (47M+)**, highlighting strong dependence on mass-market card users.

- Businessmen generate the highest revenue (**18M**), followed by White-collar customers (**10M**), indicating stronger purchasing power among professional customer segments.

- Customers aged **40–60** contribute the highest revenue share, reflecting stable income levels and higher spending capacity.

- Bills and Entertainment categories together contribute more than **24M revenue**, demonstrating high recurring and lifestyle-oriented spending behavior.

- Q4 recorded the highest quarterly revenue (**14.5M**), indicating strong seasonal and festive spending impact.

- Week 53 experienced a significant **28.8% WoW revenue increase**, representing peak customer transaction activity.

- Overall card activation rate reached **57.46%**, indicating strong customer onboarding and engagement performance.

- Delinquent accounts remained relatively low at **6.06%**, reflecting healthy repayment behavior and controlled financial risk.

# Forecasting Analysis

Revenue forecasting was performed using Power BI time-series analytics based on historical weekly revenue trends.

# Forecast Highlights
- Week 54 revenue is projected at approximately 1.05M
- Revenue trend indicates stable long-term business growth
- Seasonal spending patterns are expected to continue influencing future revenue performance
- Premium card segments and online transactions show strong future growth potential

# Business Recommendations

- Increase Gold and Platinum card adoption through premium loyalty and rewards programs.

- Target high-value customer segments such as businessmen and salaried professionals using personalized marketing campaigns.

- Maximize revenue during peak seasonal periods through promotional offers and cashback campaigns.

- Encourage online transaction growth by providing digital payment incentives and reward-based benefits.

- Focus on customer retention strategies for middle-aged high-spending customers.
